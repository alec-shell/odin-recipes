Simple Fried Chicken

A beautifully simple recipe. No exact measurements required! Fried Chicken is a southern staple fit for any kitchen, north or south!

Ingredients

  • 30 saltine crackers
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons dry potato flakes
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 1 egg
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups vegetable oil (for frying)

Instructions

  1. Place crackers in a large resealable plastic bag. Seal the bag, and crush the crackers with a rolling pin until coarse. Add the flour, potato flakes, seasoned salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Beat egg in a shallow dish or bowl. One by one, dredge chicken pieces in egg, then place in the crumb mixture bag, and shake until fully coated.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F.
  4. Fry chicken, turning frequently, until golden brown and juices run clear (15 to 20 minutes).
