S'mores

Nothing says campfire quite like a s'mores! Perhaps one of the cheapest and easiest desserts to make, you'll be sure to ask for s'more.

Ingredients

  • 1 large marshmallow
  • 1 graham cracker
  • 1 chocolate bar, preferrably Hershey's

Directions

  1. Break graham cracker in half, and set aside.
  2. Heat the marshmallow over an open flame until toasted.
  3. Sandwich two squares of hersheys chocolate and the toasted marshmallow between the two halves of the graham cracker.
