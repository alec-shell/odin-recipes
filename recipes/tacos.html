Chorizo Street Tacos

What better way to end the day than these simple chorizo street tacos? Quick and easy to make, they're sure to blow you away with flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 chorizo sausage link, casing removed and crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion (to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro (to taste)

Directions

  1. Combine chorizo, chipotle peppers and adobo sauce in a bowl.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add chorizo mixture and cook until crisp. (5 to 7 minutes)
  3. Transfer cooked chorizo mixture to a plate, retaining grease in skillet.
  4. Heat tortillas in grease until warm and slightly crisp. (1 to 2 minutes per side)
  5. fill each tortilla with chorizo mixture, onion and cilantro
